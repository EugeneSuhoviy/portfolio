About

Building the frontend layer of commerce

My career is built on one belief: the frontend is where business strategy meets user reality. Over 11 years I've specialized in the eCommerce layer — where milliseconds of load time, accessibility compliance, and clean component architecture translate directly into conversion and trust.

At Overdose, I led frontend architecture for headless Magento 2 projects — decoupling the storefront from the monolith, migrating to React / Next.js with GraphQL, and establishing patterns used across the team. I've worked across the full eCommerce stack: Shopify, Contentful, Vue/Nuxt/Hygraph, and large-scale M2 multi-store builds.

I hold Adobe Certified Expert (Magento Commerce Frontend) and Contentful Certified Professional credentials — reflecting depth, not just breadth.
